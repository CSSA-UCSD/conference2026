About:

First Spark

At Lanterns of Innovation: Exploring The Many Sides of Cognition, we explore how different fields of cognitive science come together to illuminate the mind. From psychology and AI to neuroscience and linguistics, each perspective offers a unique way of understanding how we think, learn, and interact with the world.

Hosted by CSSA, this year marks the 19th National Cognitive Science Conference, bringing together students, researchers, and professionals to share ideas, research, and experiences.

Join us in building connections and contributing to a shared vision that continues to grow brighter.
