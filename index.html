About:

First Spark

At Lanterns of Innovation, we explore how different fields of cognitive science come together to illuminate the mind. From psychology and AI to neuroscience and linguistics, each perspective offers a unique way of understanding how we think and interact with the world.

Hosted by CSSA, this marks the 19th year of bringing together students and professionals to share research and experiences.
